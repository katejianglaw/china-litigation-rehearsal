# Claim Elements

Use this reference when claims, defenses, remedies, or legal relationships need to be tested.

## Claim Decomposition

Do not stop at `诉讼请求 / 事实依据 / 法律依据`. Convert every major claim or defense into:

`请求权基础 -> 构成要件 -> 待证事实 -> 证据链接 -> 对方抗辩 -> 阻断方案`

## Main Table

| 主张/抗辩 | 请求权基础或法律关系 | 构成要件 | 待证事实 | 证据链接 | 对方可能拆解点 | 阻断方案 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

If the legal basis is not supplied or not verified, mark:

`请求权基础需检索核验；以下仅按通常分析框架拆解。`

## Element Status

Classify each element:

- `已相对扎实`: supported by direct evidence or strong chain evidence.
- `可通过解释补强`: evidence exists but inference is needed.
- `存在明显缺口`: current record may fail on this element.
- `法律适用待核验`: cannot determine without authority research.

## Defense And Blocking Plan

For each likely defense:

| 对方抗辩 | 攻击的构成要件 | 成立前提 | 我方证据/解释 | 庭前动作 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Focus on blocking the opponent's path, not merely repeating the user's preferred conclusion.

## Remedies And Amount

For money claims, require:

- amount components;
- calculation formula;
- calculation period;
- evidence for each input;
- causation;
- mitigation or avoidable loss issues;
- overlap or double recovery risks;
- fallback amount if the main calculation is not accepted.

Output:

| 金额项目 | 计算口径 | 证据 | 争议点 | 备用口径 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
