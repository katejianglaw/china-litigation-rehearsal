# Legal Authority Verification

Use this reference whenever a task needs statutes, judicial interpretations, article numbers, guiding cases, typical cases, local rules, or case-law analogies.

## Core Rule

Do not treat bundled references as final legal authority. They are retrieval clues. Before citing a legal basis externally, verify:

1. title;
2. issuing authority;
3. promulgation and effective dates;
4. current validity;
5. exact article number and text;
6. whether the rule applies to the case type and procedural stage.

If any item is not verified, write `需人工核验` or `需检索核验`.

## Source Hierarchy

| Level | Source | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 国家法律法规数据库: https://flk.npc.gov.cn/ | Laws, administrative regulations, judicial interpretations, formal validity checks |
| 2 | 最高人民法院官网: https://www.court.gov.cn/ | SPC judicial interpretations, authoritative releases, trial guidance, typical cases |
| 3 | 人民法院案例库: https://rmfyalk.court.gov.cn/ | Reference cases selected by the court system |
| 4 | 中国裁判文书网: https://wenshu.court.gov.cn/ | Similar-case research and reasoning patterns, not binding rules |
| 5 | Treatises, articles, books, CLE materials | Background methodology only, never final authority |

## Mandatory Verification Triggers

Verify before relying on:

- jurisdiction, arbitration agreement, exclusive jurisdiction, agreement jurisdiction;
- limitation period, exclusion period, suspension/interruption;
- evidence period, late evidence, evidence preservation, investigation order;
- preservation, appraisal, audit, service, default trial;
- proof burden allocation and proof standard;
- claim basis, elements, defenses, remedies, and amount calculation;
- liquidated damages adjustment, loss causation, mitigation, attorney fees;
- company resolution validity, shareholder rights, equity transfer, authorization chain;
- any article number, judicial interpretation, guiding case, typical case, conference minutes, local court view, or appellate standard.

## Citation Discipline

- Do not say `根据第X条必然...` unless article text has been verified in the current task.
- Prefer `通常需要核验的依据包括...` when working from unverified memory.
- If a case is from a judgment database, state that it is persuasive reference only, not a binding rule.
- If a rule may have changed after amendments, state the amendment risk and require current-text verification.
