# Procedure Gate

Use this reference early in quick, standard, and deep modes. Many PRC civil/commercial cases fail or narrow before merits because of procedure, timing, jurisdiction, proof deadlines, or missing original evidence.

| 程序闸门 | 需要确认的问题 | 当前材料 | 风险等级 | 下一步 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| 仲裁条款 | 是否存在仲裁协议、仲裁管辖异议、仲裁后诉讼衔接 |  | 高/中/低 |  |

## Fast Gate Output

When time is short, output only:

| P0程序炸点 | 为什么危险 | 立即动作 |
| --- | --- | --- |

Then add:

- `可继续分析的前提`: what assumptions allow merits analysis to continue.
- `暂不展开的程序点`: lower-risk items that can wait.

## Arbitration Caution

Do not treat all arbitration-related matters as the same. If arbitration appears, classify first:

- 仲裁协议效力或管辖异议。
- 撤销仲裁裁决。
- 不予执行仲裁裁决。
- 仲裁后另行诉讼或关联诉讼。
- 仲裁保全或证据保全衔接。

If the category is unclear, ask before giving procedural conclusions.

## Limitation Caution

Do not confidently determine limitation or exclusion periods without verified dates and legal basis. If central, write:

`时效/期间判断需结合请求权基础、起算点、中止中断事由及现行规则核验；当前仅作庭前风险提示。`
